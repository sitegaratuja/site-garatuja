Who we are

Garatuja transforms lives through the art of movement.

The Garatuja Institute is a non-governmental, non-profit organization that since 2006 has promoted the development of girls and women between the ages of 7 and 25 who are in situations of social vulnerability, through an interdimensional education with a focus on art, dance and citizenship.

Vision, Mission & Our Values

Mission

To democratize access to dance and promote citizenship, aiming at the human development of girls and women, through interdimensional education using art as a movement for social transformation.

Vision

To serve 500 girls in the community of São Sebastião-DF and become a national reference for social impact initiatives through dance.

Values

    Art and citizenship, Well-being and happiness, Ethics and transparency, Responsibility and professionalism, Generosity and unity
